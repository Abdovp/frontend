# Pages & Structure

## Main Pages
- Homepage
- Product/Landing Page (باك ضد سخونية الطوموبيل)
- Collection Page
- About Us
- Contact Us
- Cart Drawer
- Checkout Popup
- Thank You Page

## Homepage Structure
1. Hero section (brand, offer, CTA)
2. Proof bar (social proof)
3. Product highlight
4. Benefits/features
5. UGC/testimonials
6. CTA repeated
7. Footer (menus, contact, about)

## Product Page Structure
- Heading, subheading, stars
- Scarcity bar
- Offer cards (1/2 pieces)
- CTA (add to cart, open cart)
- Cross-sell section
- Reviews, FAQ
- Images (3-4)
- Section alternation (text/image)

## Cart Drawer
- List of products
- Cross-sell offers
- Checkout CTA

## Checkout Popup
- Name, address, phone (Moroccan validation)
- Order summary
- Social proof, scarcity
- CTA (submit order)
- Upsell popup (10-15s, 99dh)

## Thank You Page
- Order confirmation
- Social proof
- Next steps

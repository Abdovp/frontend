# Design System & UI Guidelines

## Brand Colors
- Primary: #1A73E8 (Royal Blue)
- Accent: #F9A825 (Gold)
- Neutral: #222 (Dark Gray), #fff (White)

## Logo
- BOYA (bold, modern font)
- SHOP (lighter/smaller)
- Arabic: بويا شوب
- English: Boya Shop (below Arabic)

## Header
- Right: shopping bag (circle, brand color)
- Next: logo (text)
- Menu, then cart

## Homepage
- Hero section: text right, image left
- Proof bar: social proof
- Product highlight
- Benefits/features
- UGC/testimonials
- CTA repeated
- Footer: all menus, contact, about

## Product Page
- Offer cards (1/2 pieces)
- Scarcity bar
- CTA (add to cart, open cart)
- Cross-sell section
- Reviews, FAQ
- Images: 3-4 per product
- Section alternation: text/image

## Cart & Checkout
- Drawer with cross-sell
- Checkout popup: name, address, phone
- Order summary, social proof, scarcity
- Upsell popup (10-15s, 99dh)
- Thank you page

## Responsiveness
- Mobile-first, then desktop
- All sections adapt to screen

- Replace with real images later
